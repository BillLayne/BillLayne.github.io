About Alamance Farmers' Mutual

A trusted North Carolina insurance partner since 1892

Our Heritage

Founded in 1892, Alamance Farmers' Mutual Insurance Company has been proudly serving North Carolina for over 130 years. As a mutual insurance company, we're owned by our policyholders, not shareholders, which means our primary focus is on serving our members' needs.

Our Mission

We exist to serve North Carolinians exclusively, understanding the unique risks and needs of our state. Our deep local knowledge and commitment to the community sets us apart from national carriers.
